The Creator formed the first Rats from the dust of the earth, and imbued them with the Spark of life. Over time, the Creator watched as the Rats evolved and adapted to their surroundings, guiding their evolution and shaping them into the first human ancestors. The Creator chose the Rat as the Vessel for the creation of humanity.
